What We Build

A Neural Network That Runs on Light

Levu's photonic integrated circuits perform matrix multiplication, nonlinear activation, and layer-to-layer propagation entirely in the optical domain — with power consumption orders of magnitude below conventional silicon.

01
Optical Matrix Multiply

Fully reversible nonlinear optical interactions compute any linear transformation in a single pass — no multiply-accumulate units needed.

02
Nonlinear Activation

The activation function emerges from the optical interactions themselves — no code, no digital logic, no extra hardware.

03
WDM Layer Stacking

Wavelength-division multiplexing runs multiple neural network layers on the same waveguide simultaneously — depth at zero additional cost.

Why Levu

Light Does What Silicon Cannot

Every conventional AI chip is bottlenecked by the same physics: electrons moving through resistive material generate heat, consume power, and max out bandwidth. Scaling means more chips, more cooling, more energy.

Photons carry no charge. They don't generate heat when they compute. A single waveguide carries hundreds of independent wavelength channels simultaneously.

Our fully reversible nonlinear optical platform is the first to bring true end-to-end optical training — forward and backward passes in the same hardware — to a manufacturable silicon photonics process.
